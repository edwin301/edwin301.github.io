Frequently Asked Questions

Can you handle tight deadlines?

We can accommodate tight deadlines, depending on the project scope. Let us know your timeline, and we’ll do our best to deliver on time.

What will be the final output?

We will provide fully Layered FIGMA files as a final output ready for the developers to code.

What's the work process?

1. We’ll design the first page and send a PNG for feedback. 2. Then revise it based on your input until you’re satisfied. 3. Once approved, we’ll deliver the final files.

Is your work mobile responsive?

It is fully responsive for mobile, tablets & normal screens

What if I need help after the project is finished?

We offer free support after project completion, so feel free to reach out anytime for assistance. For major changes to your order, additional charges may apply.

What’s the turnaround time?

The timeframe depends on the scope. Typically, we deliver the first draft of the landing page design within 1 to 5 days
